Interesting Facts

  1. Establishment Date: South Carolina was founded on March 26, 1663, by King Charles II of England.

  2. Revolutionary History: South Carolina played a pivotal role in the American Revolutionary War, witnessing the first decisive victory for the colonies in the Battle of Sullivan's Island in 1776.

  3. Civil War Significance: The Civil War began with the first shots fired at Fort Sumter in Charleston Harbor on April 12, 1861, after South Carolina became the first state to secede from the Union on December 20, 1860.

  4. Charleston's Historic District: Charleston, the oldest city in South Carolina, boasts a well-preserved historic district featuring cobblestone streets, antebellum mansions, and iconic sites like Rainbow Row.

  5. Economic Profile: South Carolina's median household income is slightly below the national average, with recent data showing around $56,000. However, the state's lower cost of living contributes to its appeal.
